Available Market Adjustment

Available market represents the number of high school graduates projected to go to college that are available to you, based on the college going rate and the number of students going out of state/staying in state for each state. These projections are updated annually.

Use the What If Available Market adjustment tool if any of the markets you are focusing on experience significant changes over the course of the year.

To use, simply lower the percentage in the teal box of the appropriate state by the percentage of decline in college going rate that you anticipate.

Example: If recent news shows your home state anticipating a 4% decline in college going rate, enter 96% in the teal box for that state and the projection will adjust accordingly.
